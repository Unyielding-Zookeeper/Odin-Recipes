Lasagna

Description

Imagine a warm, hearty lasagna with layers of tender pasta, rich tomato sauce, seasoned ground beef, and a creamy blend of ricotta and mozzarella cheese.

The top layer is perfectly golden and slightly crispy, creating a comforting dish that’s both simple and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 24 ounces tomato sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozerella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook Noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain.
  3. Cook Beef: Brown the ground beef in a skillet, season with salt and pepper, then drain excess fat.
  4. Assemble Lasagna: In a baking dish, layer 3 noodles, then spread a third of the tomato sauce, a third of the beef, a third of the ricotta, and a third of the mozzarella. Repeat twice.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes until the cheese is golden.
  6. Serve: Let it cool slightly before cutting into squares and serving.
